Our Ships

OUR company use Container Ships: These are specialized vessels designed to carry standardized shipping containers. They have a large carrying capacity and are used for transporting a wide range of goods in containers, including manufactured products, raw materials, and consumer goods. Bulk Carriers: Bulk carriers are used to transport bulk commodities such as coal, iron ore, grain, and other similar cargoes. They are designed with large cargo holds and are equipped with specialized equipment for efficient loading and unloading.
